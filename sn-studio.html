How we work

From idea to launch

A clear process, no surprises. You always know which stage the project is at.

01

Discovery

We dig into your idea, define goals, audience, and key features. We craft the spec together.

02

Design

Figma prototypes, UX testing, design system. Every screen is approved before development starts.

03

Development

1–2 week sprints, weekly demos, CI/CD. You see progress in real time.

04

Launch
